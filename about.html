ABOUT US

Welcome to our Water Rafting Adventure! Established in 2022, our business is nestled in the heart of Lodwar, Loturerei, Kenya. We are dedicated to delivering unforgettable water rafting experiences on some of the region's most thrilling and scenic river routes. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned rafter, we provide tailored adventures that connect you with the natural beauty and vibrant culture of Lodwar. Join us for an exhilarating journey and make memories on the wild waters of Kenya!
